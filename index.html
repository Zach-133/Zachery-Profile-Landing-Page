About
Engineering taught me to think in systems. AI automation taught me what's possible when those systems can think back.

I'm Zachery. Mechanical Engineer by training, AI Automation Engineer by conviction.

It started with personal pain points. Small automations running quietly in the background, streamlining my daily routine, tracking my workouts, keeping me organised. Then a job search tool to help my girlfriend find her role in London. Each project taught me something the last one couldn't.

Now, I use my experience to build agentic workflows. My flagship project, Tello, is a SaaS voice interview coach launching April 2026. It conducts industry-researched mock interviews via a voice agent, grades responses through a weighted rubric, and converts probabilistic AI outputs into deterministic, actionable scores.

I'm drawn to ambitious startups where ownership and accountability are valued, surrounded by peers who are shaping how AI agents will transform the way teams operate. If these values align with yours, I'd love to join you.
